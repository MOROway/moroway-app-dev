🚉 Züge:
Sie können sieben Züge in zwei Kreisen steuern.

🕹️ Usage:
Control MOROway's trains by using the transformer on the right side. Select a train with the toggle on the left side. Alternatively you may simply click on the desired train or use the train control center.

🏎️ Cars:
The three cars can be operated separately or move automatically.

🌆 3D:
As an alternative to the bird's eye view there is a simple 3D view.

More features:
🔉 Listen to the trains with sound effects.
👁️ Relax in demo mode.
🎮 Play with friends using multiplayer mode.
🖼️ Zoom and tilt (3D) with gestures (touch, mouse, keyboard).
🎥 Follow trains and cars (3D).
